Book End Privacy Policy

This document outlines how the application Book End makes use of your data. It also explains how a user can delete / revoke any data.

On device data

When using the application, information about books and reading sessions are stored on your device locally. These remain private (on device) never contact any external servers.

The following information is stored on your device:

Books

    Author Book Description Genre Image Cover Pages Current Pages Total
  • Title

Reading Sessions

    Start Time / Date End Time / Date Pages read during session Reading speed (pages per hour) Duration of reading session

Photo Access

Photo Library access is required in order to import images of books. To revoke this, please do so in the iOS Privacy Settings.

No image data is ever transfered off your device

Removing Data

To remove indivual data, please delete the associated books / reading sessions from your device

To delete ALL DATA please delete the entire app.

For any concerns please contact me via email at: help.bookend@gmail.com.
